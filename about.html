About Me

The Basics (+/-)

My name is Amelia Oon, and I'm a nineteen year old college student at Northeastern University. My major is Computer Science and Interactive Media. I grew up in a suburb of Scranton, Pennsylvania, but I'm enjoying my time in the city of Boston!

My Work (+/-)

Currently, I'm focusing on web design. I work with HTML and CSS and am currently teaching myself Javascript. At the moment, I have a job with Northeastern University's Pharmasceutical Department designing a professor's lab website and helping out updating the department website.

In the future, I hope to learn back-end development and mobile dev. I'm planning on learning Ruby/Rails and SQL after I finish Javascript. After I graduate, I hope to have a web dev job at either a cool tech company or startup.

If you're in need of a front end designer, contact me! I'm always looking for new projects.

My Projects(+/-)

Recently I went to a hackathon called Hack Beanpot. There, I worked on a project with my friends called Echodrop. It is a mobile app that allows you to drop audio on a location so that others can pick it up and listen to it. We're continuing its development even after the hackathon and plan to release it to the Android app store, and eventually the Apple app store as well. I made the website for this app, and will continue to develop it. You can find out more about our project here.

I am also constantly developing my personal website. If you check back in a month or two, it will probably either have different features, or it will look fairly different from how it looks now.
